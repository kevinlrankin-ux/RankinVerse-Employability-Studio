## Questions

### 1. Which statement best describes sexual harassment in this module?
Type: Multiple choice

Options:
- Any workplace disagreement
- Unwelcome conduct of a sexual nature that may affect work conditions or the work environment
- Any comment made during a break
- A performance issue unrelated to conduct

Correct answer:
- Unwelcome conduct of a sexual nature that may affect work conditions or the work environment

---

### 2. What does unwelcome conduct mean in this context?
Type: Multiple choice

Options:
- Conduct that is clearly invited and accepted
- Conduct that is unwanted and inappropriate in the workplace context
- Conduct required by a supervisor
- Conduct that only happens outside work

Correct answer:
- Conduct that is unwanted and inappropriate in the workplace context

---

### 3. Which of the following should be reported through the appropriate workplace channel?
Type: Checkbox

Options:
- repeated unwelcome sexual comments
- inappropriate touching
- hostile or offensive conduct based on a protected characteristic
- retaliation after reporting misconduct
- routine task instructions from a supervisor

Correct answers:
- repeated unwelcome sexual comments
- inappropriate touching
- hostile or offensive conduct based on a protected characteristic
- retaliation after reporting misconduct

---

### 4. What is retaliation?
Type: Multiple choice

Options:
- A normal scheduling change requested by the employee
- Negative action because a person reported misconduct or participated in a process
- A standard performance review
- A required workplace training

Correct answer:
- Negative action because a person reported misconduct or participated in a process
